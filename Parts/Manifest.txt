MDF-G3.cdr:  
  CorelDraw file with all parts for printing to 24"x18" sheets.  
  The first two pages are all the required parts.  Remaining sheets are
  extra parts and don't need to be cut.

  Laser Cutter was set to cut 1/4" MDF.

*.dxf: 
  Individual parts for laser cutter.

Traveller.stl:
  The traveller that moves along the rail for 3D printing.
